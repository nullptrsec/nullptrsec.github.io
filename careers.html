CNO Developer (Android)

Job Description

This job posting is for a full-time CNO Developer embedded in the customer-site. You will work with other contractors and customer engineers to deliver impactful, mission-critical capabilities. The duties required by this position covers the full-spectrum of software development: Planning, Programming, Testing, Shipping. Projects can range from traditional software-engineering following the software development lifecycle, to focused vulnerability research and exploitation development. You must be able to work independently, and as a part of a team.

This job requires a TS/SCI or higher, and the customer-site is in Chantilly, VA.

Required Skills

  • 3 years of C/C++ development experience
  • 3 years of Linux development experience
  • Experience in kernel and tool development for Android platforms
  • Python experience
  • Basic networking knowledge (TCP/IP, UDP, HTTP)
  • Familiarity testing C/C++ and Python software

Preferred Skills

  • Understanding of exploitation techniques
  • Proficiency in reverse-engineering software and vulnerability research
  • Proficiency in networking and communications
  • Previous experience writing CNO Tools/Capabilities
  • Previous experience writing Java and Kotlin Android applications
